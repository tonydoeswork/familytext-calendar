# FamilyText Calendar

**Anthony Anderson**

FamilyText Calendar is a private household SMS application that allows registered family members to manage a shared calendar entirely through text messages.

This is a personal application operated by Anthony Anderson for household use only. It is not a commercial product and is not available to the general public.

---

## How It Works

Registered users send natural language text messages to a dedicated phone number. The application processes the message and adds events to, or retrieves events from, a shared family Google Calendar.

Examples:
- "Dentist Thursday 2pm" → adds event to calendar
- "What's today?" → returns today's schedule
- "HELP" → returns usage guide

---

## How to Join

FamilyText Calendar is a private system for household members only. To request access, contact Anthony Anderson directly. Once your number has been added to the system, you will receive a welcome message confirming your enrollment.

After receiving your welcome message, text **START** to **(612) 431-8751** to confirm your opt-in and begin receiving messages.

By texting START, you agree to receive automated SMS messages from FamilyText Calendar including calendar confirmations, query responses, and daily schedule summaries. Message frequency varies based on your activity. Message and data rates may apply. Text **STOP** at any time to unsubscribe. Text **HELP** for assistance.
